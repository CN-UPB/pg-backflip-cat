About

The goal of this project group is to collect those datasets for a diverse collection of different VNFs and network services. To perform and automate this collection process, existing measurement tools, like the 5GTANGO NFV benchmarking tool, will be used and extended within this project. Finally, the collected datasets need to be polished and analysed before they are made available for the research community.
